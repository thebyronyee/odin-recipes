Steamed Buns Recipe

Steamed buns or "bao" in Chinese are a common dish in Chinese cuisine and can be eaten for breakfast, lunch, or even dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 2 tsp Yeast
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • 2 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 1/8 tsp Salt

Directions

  1. Mix warm milk, sugar, and yeast together. Let sit until yeast is activated; foam should appear at the top of the milk (about 5 min.).
  2. Mix in flour and salt.
  3. Cover and let rise until double in size (about 30-45 min.).
  4. Option: Add other fillings should as meat, beans, or custard
  5. Steam in a rack for about 20min.
  6. Remove from the stove, but do NOT uncover immediately. Let rest anoth 10 min. before eating.
