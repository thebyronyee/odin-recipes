How to Make "Mburu Tapalapa" Senegalese Bread

Mburu tapala neex na. Bimaa neekee Senegal, bess bu neek mburu laa ndekki.

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups flour
  • 3 tsp Yeast (1 packet)
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 1/2 Cup Warm Water

Directions

  1. Add and mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Slowly add water and mix until dough-like consistency.
  3. Cover with dry cloth and let rise until doubled in size (about 1 hour).
  4. Cut and roll in to 3 loaves. Use a knife to make a shallow cut along the top center of each loaf.
  5. Let rise until double in size again (about 30 min).
  6. Brush tops lightly with water right before going into the oven.
  7. Bake at 450 degrees F for about 15 min. or until the crust is golden brown.
  8. Eat It!
